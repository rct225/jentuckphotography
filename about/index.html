Inspiration.

It can strike when you least expect it. For some, it's the result of a word, a feeling or even an experience. For me, and for my photography, inspiration does not manifest itself in simply images but rather moments.

A few recent sources of inspiration: a proud aunt doting over her newborn niece, a couple who have been together for years but act as if they're newlyweds, a family doing what they do best -- having fun.

As I continue to evolve as a photographer, I've discovered that some of my favorite images aren't posed or planned. They're the silly giggles, the pouty faces and the stolen kisses. They're moments.

I invite you to browse my website and reach out to learn more about my photography and how I can help you capture your own moments.
